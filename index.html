01 — Who I am

Builder. Researcher.
Curious human.

I'm a Master's student in Human-Computer Interaction at Uppsala University, currently in my first year and already deep in the questions that matter most to me: how do we design AI systems that humans can genuinely understand, trust, and relate to?

Before academia, I spent three years as a software developer at IBM — shipping enterprise-grade web applications, wrestling with real-world product constraints, and learning that the gap between a good idea and a working system is where everything interesting happens.

What drew me to research is the same thing that drew me to coding: a need to understand systems from the inside out. Now I combine that engineering instinct with rigorous human-centered research methods — running studies with the Furhat social robot, investigating bias in generative AI, and analyzing how anthropomorphic design shapes user behaviour.

I'm based in Uppsala, originally from India, and currently exploring what it means to do a PhD at the frontier of HRI and agentic AI — specifically in assistive robotics for people who need it most.

03 — Research work

Projects at the edge
of human & machine

🤖
Empathy in Social Robots: A Furhat Study
Designed a comparative study (Empathetic vs. Neutral robot conditions) to evaluate how anthropomorphic behaviour influences user motivation. Implemented conversational flows using the Furhat SDK and Python, prompt-engineering the robot to display warmth and active listening. Measured perceived safety with the Godspeed Questionnaire.
↳ Furhat SDK · Python · Godspeed Questionnaire · Ethics Analysis
⚖️
Mitigating Bias in Generative AI
Investigated bias in large-scale training datasets and implemented data cleaning strategies before model training. Designed evaluation frameworks to assess fairness and reduce algorithmic bias in model outputs. Conducted at KTH Royal Institute.
↳ LLM Evaluation · Data Quality · Fairness Frameworks
🔬
AI Agent Trust Calibration at Gaddr AB
Ongoing industry research on how users develop trust (or distrust) in AI agents. Analysing user behaviour metrics to identify friction points in AI feature adoption and examining how anthropomorphic design choices affect agency attribution.
↳ Trust Research · Qualitative + Quantitative · Industry Setting
📊
Computational Social Science: Screen Time & Sleep
Analysed a Kaggle dataset on teenage social media usage and sleep patterns using Orange Data Mining. Conducted statistical correlation analysis and sentiment analysis linking high screen time to behavioural health metrics.
↳ Orange Data Mining · Sentiment Analysis · Statistical Methods
🏆
Public Sector Data Vis — UU Hackathon Winner
Redesigned public sector data visualisations for Uppsala Kommun to improve accessibility for non-technical citizens. Built interactive dashboards in Flourish and Figma. Awarded 1st prize at the UU AI Innovation Hackathon 2025.
↳ Flourish · Figma · Data Accessibility · 1st Prize
💜
Participatory Design for Uppsala Women's Shelter
Led co-design workshops with vulnerable stakeholders using ethical participatory methods. Designed safer awareness and communication tools for bystanders, with a trauma-informed research ethics approach throughout.
↳ Participatory Design · Vulnerable Populations · Ethics
